Bar Operations Manual

Introduction

The following document contains three levels of classic cocktails which one would expect to be able to make in any bar of quality.

Please study Level One and the Minimalist and Maximalist drinks first in order to be prepared to bartend. Levels Two and Three may be studied and completed at your own pace.
